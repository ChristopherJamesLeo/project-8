Nitor Group Ltd originally established in 1982 in the UK, comprised multi-disciplined consulting engineers specialising in the design and construction of firearms training ranges. At the outset, services included the full study of needs analysis to determine training requirements; schematic and outline proposals; detailed design, equipment specifications; tender production and contract supervision.

In response to the customer demand, the company evolved into a Prime Contractor for specialised training facilities involving complex turnkey operations, including the supply and fit-out of specialist materials, training solutions and maintenance programmes.
In 1999, Nitor Group Ltd was purchased by Advanced Interactive Systems Inc (Nitor), Seattle a global company providing training and technology in the security, law enforcement and defence marketplaces. Nitor combined state-of-the-art technology and extensive tactical experience to provide immersive, real-life interactive simulated training systems.

Cubic Global Defense (Cubic) a division of Cubic Corporation a leading provider of Combat Training Systems, Cyber Technologies, Defense Electronics and Mission Support Services in 2013 acquired Advanced Interactive Solutions Pte Ltd (AIS).
Nitor Defence Pte Ltd (Nitor) in Singapore, founded in September 2019 now leverages upon 21 years of Global Experience designing & delivering world class ‘live fire’ training facilities, shooting ranges, close quarter battle and specialised training solutions to provide customers with a ‘trusted’ one stop solution.
